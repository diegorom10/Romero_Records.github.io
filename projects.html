Most Famous Song

Take me to church [2014]

“Take Me to Church” is Hozier’s first big single from his debut album. This is one of the dangerously beautiful music video ever made. The deeply emotional song talks about the oppression, indoctrination and discrimination done by Church in the name of religion. “Homophobia is one just aspect of this bigger concept”. Inspired from this piece ,there is also a beautiful ballet performance

by Sergei Polunin.
